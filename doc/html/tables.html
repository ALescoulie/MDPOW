mdpow.tables.solvent_molecular_volume = {'water': 0.0301475 (5.52147e-06), 'water_296': 0.0301174 (4.05405e-06), 'octanol': 0.263893 (7.66129e-05)}¶

volume of a solvent molecule at standard conditions, in nm^3

mdpow.tables.solvent_selections = {'water': 'r SOL & a OW', 'octanol': 'r 1OCT & a OH'}¶

make_ndx commands to select either the OW in TIP4P water (key water) or OH in 1-octanol (octanol). This is rather specific for mdpow because we assume that the residue name is “SOL” for water or “1OCT” for octanol. These commands are used in count_solvent_molecules().
